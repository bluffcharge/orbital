Orbital — the component system

A high-contrast, neon-on-navy interface language: flat white surfaces, hard offset shadows, square corners, and one electric accent. Every component here is live — rendered from the system's own CSS and JS — organized by the surfaces it builds: foundations, campaign-management controls, the selection carousel, profile & data cards, animated globes, chat & scoring, and landing heroes. Interact with anything; this is the playground before it ports to Storybook.
